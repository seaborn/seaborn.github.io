Citing seaborn¶

If seaborn is integral to a scientific publication, please cite it. A paper describing seaborn has been published in the Journal of Open Source Software. Here is a ready-made BibTeX entry:

@article{Waskom2021,
    doi = {10.21105/joss.03021},
    url = {https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.03021},
    year = {2021},
    publisher = {The Open Journal},
    volume = {6},
    number = {60},
    pages = {3021},
    author = {Michael L. Waskom},
    title = {seaborn: statistical data visualization},
    journal = {Journal of Open Source Software}
 }

In most situations where seaborn is cited, a citation to matplotlib would also be appropriate.
